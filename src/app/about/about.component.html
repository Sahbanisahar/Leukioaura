About the hospital

The oldest hospital in Tunis, it was founded in 1662 under the reign of the Mouradite dynasty. Originally called Bimaristan Al Azzafine (Bimaristan of musicians), it is located in the El Azzafine souk, a territory retained as inalienable property by Princess Aziza Othmana. The hospital building still exists, at number 101 rue de la Kasbah,
